About Me

My name is Rob, I am an aspiring developer currently working for Apple and living in Limerick, Ireland. My journey into Software Development evolved from my love of design and freelance work as an Illustrator and Graphic Designer. For several years, I worked as a freelancer which gave me the chance to grow and learn from my favorite artists and to explore new areas of design.

I'm always searching for new ways to be creative. I started making and editing music both with bands 'Last Days of Death Country' in 2012, and 'Bury Me at Sea' in 2018, as well as my own. In early 2019, I started a Full Stack Developer course online with Code Institute, studying to achieve the foundations and tools to help me secure the role of a web developer.
